Welcome to the 2018 Quantum Material Summer School

General informations

The 2018 CIFAR Quantum Materials Summer School will be held in Montreal on May 28 - 30, 2018. The school is intended for graduate students and postdocs, and will cover a range of topics in preparation for the main CIFAR Quantum Materials Meeting that will be held in Sherbrooke on May 31 - June 1, 2018 right after the summer school. The school will consist of 1 and 2 hours lectures by local and guest experts, a poster session and much more!

Who should attend

Registration is reserved for students/postdocs of CIFAR Quantum Materials members and invited EPiQS investigators. There is a maximum of 80 students and postdoc participants from CIFAR Quantum Materials members. An additional 22 spots are reserved for invited EPiQS investigators. In the initial round of applications, each CIFAR Quantum Materials Program member and EPiQS investigator can propose a maximum of two students. If spots remain after the initial round, additional students may be submitted by members.

Registration fees

The cost of attending the Summer School is $300.00 per participant.

The cost of attending the Summer School and Program Meeting is $600.00 per participant.

After the summer school, eligible travel and meal expenses are reimbursed through CIFAR using the expense claim form.

For more informations, take a look at the travel policy.

Local informations

The summer school will be held at the Marriott Hotel.

The cheapest way from the airport to downtown Montreal is public transit.

From the downtown bus station (Beri-UQAM), the easiest way to the hotel is to dropoff at station Place-des-arts on the green subway line.

Breakfasts, lunches and coffee will be provided throughout the event. Self organize meals will be reimbursed by CIFAR. (travel policy)

Schedule

Participants are expected to arrive at the Summer School on Sunday May 27, 2018 evening. The summer school starts the next morning. Students who are only attending the Summer School are expected to depart on the evening of Wednesday May 30. Students who are staying to attend the main meeting are expected to depart on the evening of Saturday June 2. If you have alternate arrival and departure times, please be sure to indicate this on registration.
